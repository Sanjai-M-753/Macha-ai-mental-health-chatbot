AI mental health chatbot for students with Thanglish conversations, stress-relief games, screening tools, and counselling support.
Macha AI 🤖💙
Your Friendly Mental Health Buddy for Students

ChillMacha AI is a bilingual AI mental health chatbot designed for college students in India.
It provides friendly conversations, stress-relief activities, and mental health screening in a natural Thanglish (Tamil + English) style.

The goal is to make mental health support easy, anonymous, and stigma-free.

🌟 Key Features
💬 Conversational AI Chatbot

Friendly Thanglish conversation style

Talks like a supportive college friend

Uses humour and motivational replies

🧠 Mental Health Screening

The chatbot conducts validated screening tools:

PHQ-9 – Depression screening

GAD-7 – Anxiety screening

C-SSRS – Suicide risk detection

These are delivered as natural conversations instead of clinical questionnaires.

🌍 Multilingual Support

Users can interact in multiple languages.

Primary language:

Thanglish (Tamil + English mix)

🎮 Stress Relief Section

The platform also includes:

Anti-stress mini games

Fun interaction elements

Positive motivational messages

Designed to reduce stress and improve mood.

🚨 Crisis Support

If the chatbot detects signs of severe distress or suicidal ideation, it provides immediate support resources:

iCall Helpline

Vandrevala Foundation

Other mental health helplines

This ensures users are guided towards real professional help.

🔒 Privacy & Safety

ChillMacha AI is designed with user privacy in mind.

Anonymous conversations

No personal data required

Safe messaging practices

Mental health awareness focus
